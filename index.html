Welcome to Afghan Proverbs

Afghan proverbs carry centuries of wisdom. Discover the soul of Afghan culture through these sayings.

Featured Proverbs

کوه به کوه نمی‌رسد، آدم به آدم می‌رسد

Mountains don’t meet, but people do.

It means people will eventually meet again, even if unexpectedly.

از آب گل‌آلود ماهی گرفتن.

Fishing in muddy water.

Taking advantage of a messy or chaotic situation for personal gain.

Proverb Gallery

صبر تلخ است ولی بر شیرین دارد

Patience is bitter, but it bears sweet fruit.

It means that although being patient is difficult, the result is good in the end.

Proverb of the Day

باد آورده را باد می‌برد

What the wind brings, the wind takes away.

Meaning: Easy gains are easily lost.
