صبر تلخ است ولی بر شیرین دارد

Patience is bitter, but it bears sweet fruit.

It means that although being patient is difficult, the result is good in the end.

یک دست صدا ندارد

One hand has no sound.

Meaning: Teamwork is essential for success.

باد آورده را باد می‌برد

What the wind brings, the wind takes away.

Meaning: Easy gains are easily lost.

هر که نان از عمل خویش خورد، منت حاتم طایی نبرد

He who eats from his own labor owes no debt to Hatam Tai.

Meaning: Independence through hard work is honorable.

آب که یک‌جا ماند، می‌گندد

Water that stays in one place becomes stagnant.

Meaning: Stagnation leads to decline; keep moving and progressing.

تا مرد سخن نگفته باشد، عیب و هنرش نهفته باشد

Until a man speaks, his flaws and virtues remain hidden.

Meaning: A person’s words reveal their true character.

سنگ بزرگ، علامت نزدن است

A big stone is a sign of not hitting.

Meaning: Making grand promises often means no action will follow.

نیکی کن و در دجله انداز

Do good and throw it into the Tigris.

Meaning: Do good without expecting anything in return.

از کوزه همان برون تراود که در اوستstrong>

A pot spills only what it contains.

Meaning: One's true nature is revealed through their actions and words.
